About Us

We have always been about Customer Experience!

Gadget Gainey was established by Ashley Gainey, who was really interested in a service that no other company focuses on... the customer service side. This is a true pet peeve to Ashley if you didn't have a great experience then why would you ever want to go back to that service ever again? Ashley always liked talking to Customer Service because most of the time, they asked how he was and yeah maybe he should have said he was fine but he explained a little bit about his day and got chatting to someone that was their very first day at this big online store, they said that they hope to have someone that shared an interest in their job and in what they do every day!

His goal was to just Dropship items. Which is what we still do now, our motto is that we are Providing you with the best products with a great price! We care for our customers and for that, that is key to success. To this day, Ashley does everything, orders the items, social media ads, listings items on this site as well as items on our eBay store but also, what job does he love the most... customer service!

Unfortunately though, Ashley now has other commitments, he has a full-time job, he also studies. With having a Degree Apprenticeship, he doesn't have much time to expand, so now we, Jade and Chris have stepped up to the plate and are now filling what he does. Ashley still does a bit when he can, but we are trying to get ready for Phase 2 of Gadget Gainey which is to have a store that we can sell our products on, instead of redirecting to eBay!

So watch this space! There is a lot in store soon!
